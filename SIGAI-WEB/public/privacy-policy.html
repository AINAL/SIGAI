Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Sigai is a learning app designed to help primary school students understand multiplication and division. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Sigai collects minimal data necessary to improve your experience. We do not collect personal or sensitive user data. The information collected includes:

  • Usage Analytics: App interactions and crash reports to improve functionality.
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system version, and basic app performance metrics.
  • App Preferences: Stored locally via AppStorage, including:

      Language selection (English/Malay).

      User progress in the learning module.

2. Use of Firebase AI API

Sigai integrates **Firebase AI API** to provide AI-driven chat support within the app. This feature helps users with mathematical problem-solving. Here's how we handle this data:

  • **User Queries**: Sent to Firebase AI for processing (not stored by us).
  • **AI Responses**: Displayed in the app but not retained.
  • **No User Tracking**: We do not associate API interactions with personal identities.

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used to:

  • Improve user experience and app performance.
  • Enhance AI chat responses for better learning support.
  • Fix bugs and ensure smooth app functionality.
  • Save user-selected preferences (language, progress) for a better learning experience.

4. Data Storage & Security

Sigai ensures that user data is handled securely:

  • **AppStorage Data**: Stored locally on the user's device (not shared externally).
  • **Firebase AI API**: Data sent for AI processing is not permanently stored by Sigai.
  • **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device (via AppStorage settings).
  • Delete progress and reset preferences within the app.
  • Disable AI chat interactions if desired.
  • Contact us for privacy concerns or data requests.

6. Links to External Websites

The Sigai app provides links to external pages, including the **Terms of Use** and **Privacy Policy**, hosted on our website. These links are provided for transparency, and we encourage users to review them.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and users will be notified of significant updates.
